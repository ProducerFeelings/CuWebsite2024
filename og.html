    • Chronic Urticaria (Chronic Idiopatic Urticaria)


    Chronic urticaria, also known as chronic hives, is a condition characterized by the presence of itchy, raised welts on the skin that persist for six weeks or longer. These welts, or hives, can appear anywhere on the body and may vary in size and shape.
